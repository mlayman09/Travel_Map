-------Data Model Documentation---------


Column:  NAME

Values: {[Name of feature, State abbreviation]}  *if not a US location, just write the province or country where "state" would go. For example, "Jerusalem, Israel".




Column:  COUNTRY

Values: {Name of Country}




Column:  TYPE

Values:  {City, Town, National Park, National Forest, Wilderness Area, Natural Feature, Historical Feature}
